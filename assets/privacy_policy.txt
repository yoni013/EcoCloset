Privacy Policy and Limitation of Liability
1. Introduction
Welcome to EcoCloset (the “App,” “we,” “us,” or “our”). Our App connects buyers and sellers of second-hand clothing, providing a user-friendly platform to list, browse, and purchase items with features such as personalized recommendations and AI-assisted product tagging.
We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect your personal data when you interact with our services. It also sets out your rights under applicable data protection laws including, but not limited to, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other international regulations.
By accessing or using our App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue using the App immediately.
2. Information We Collect
2.1 Personal Data
We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, such as:
* Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number.
* Profile Preferences: Clothing size, style preferences, favorite brands.
* User-Generated Content: Listing descriptions, item photos, messages to other users.
2.2 Account and Transaction Data
* Listings and Purchases: Data related to the items you list for sale or purchase (including transaction details).
* Order History: Records of your past and current orders, returns, or cancellations.
2.3 Behavioral Data
* Browsing Behavior: Pages or products viewed, time spent on the App, saved searches.
* Interaction with Recommendations: Clicks, likes, shares, or saves on personalized suggestions.
2.4 Device and Usage Data
* Technical Information: IP addresses, browser type, device identifiers, app version.
* Analytics: Engagement metrics, crash reports, system logs, and other diagnostic data.
2.5 Payment Information
* The App does not directly process or store payment method details (such as credit card numbers).
* We do not handle payments between users. All transactions are coordinated directly between buyers and sellers using their chosen payment methods (e.g., Bit, Paybox, cash).
* We may collect limited transaction metadata (e.g., transaction amount for listings, meeting coordination details) to facilitate dispute resolution and improve our matching services.
3. How We Collect Data
3.1 Directly from You
* Sign-Up and Profile Creation: Information you provide when creating an account or updating your profile.
* Listings and Purchases: Details you submit when listing or buying items.
* User Interactions: Communication with customer support, surveys, or feedback forms.
3.2 Automatically
* Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect usage data and improve user experience.
* Analytics Tools: Third-party analytics providers help us understand user engagement and App performance.
3.3 From Third Parties
* Payment Processors: Data received from payment platforms regarding transactions, refunds, or potential fraud.
* Fraud Detection Services: Information from third parties to help detect and prevent fraudulent activities.
4. Purpose of Data Collection
We use your information for the following purposes:
1. Service Delivery and Personalization
   * To manage your account, provide the core buying and selling functionalities, and customize your experience (e.g., personalized recommendations).
2. Transaction Facilitation
   * To process listings, sales, returns, and manage payment confirmations through third-party payment processors.
3. Analytics and Improvements
   * To analyze user trends, monitor usage patterns, and enhance our App’s interface, features, and security.
4. Fraud Detection and Security
   * To verify user accounts, prevent fraud, and ensure a secure environment.
5. Legal Compliance
   * To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes, or to respond to lawful requests.
5. Data Sharing and Third-Party Disclosure
We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your data in the following circumstances:
1. Service Providers and Partners

   * Service Coordination: We may share transaction coordination details (pickup times, locations) with users to facilitate successful exchanges.
   * AI Recommendations and Tagging: Sharing relevant data with AI service providers to improve personalized suggestions.
   * Analytics Providers: Such as Google Analytics or other similar tools to help analyze how you use the App.
   2. Legal and Compliance

      * We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, or other legal process, or if necessary to protect our rights or the safety of our users.
      3. Business Transactions

         * If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, asset sale, or bankruptcy, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to appropriate confidentiality obligations.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
         1. Access and Rectification

            * Request access to and obtain a copy of your personal data.
            * Update or correct inaccurate personal information.
            2. Data Deletion

               * Request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal or contractual limitations.
               3. Opt-Out of Marketing Communications

                  * Choose not to receive promotional emails or notifications (we will still send you transactional or service-related messages).
                  4. Cookie and Tracking Preferences

                     * Adjust your cookie settings through your browser or device settings.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@ecocloset.com. We will respond to valid requests within the time required by applicable law.

We retain your data only as long as it is necessary for:
                     * Fulfilling the purposes outlined in this Policy (e.g., providing services, maintaining transaction records).
